Board the USS Beyond and Discover a New World

Go over and beyond your average vacation on the USS Beyond. You'll be whisked away on the high seas to amazing destinations where not even the skies are the limits.

Food

Onboard you will find many food courts to satisfy your taste for adventure. Let us do the cooking because you deserve to relax. Try Crazy Hibachi, an Asian eatery offering entrees from the hibachi and Mongolian grill, plus sushi rolls and sashimi. At Santo Coyote Mexican Food & Tequilla Bar, a full menu awaits you. Eat queso, taco salad, chimichangas, burritos, fajitas and other Mexican classics in colorful sourroundings. Come and get your tast buds raving at The Purple Onion. This American restaurant features fried pickles and mushrooms, chichen wings, gourmet burgers, fresh Gulf Oysters and more.
