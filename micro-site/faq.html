FAQ

Where will we cruise to?

Caribbean, Hawaii, Europe

Where does the ship depart from?

Galveston, TX; New Orleans, LA

What is the length of the cruise?

2 - 5 Days, 6 - 9 Days

What items are not allowed on the ship?

weapons, ammunition, explosives, drugs, candles, and incense, kiddie pools, surfboards, canoes, shoes with wheels

How much luggage can I bring?

1 bag per person = 2 - 5 Days, 2 bags per person = 6 - 9 Days

Will there be WiFi?

Yes. Our ships provides satellite Internet access ship-wide.

Does Galaxy have a mobile app?

Yes. Download it free from the app store.

What is Gratuitive Service?

It's customary for the staff to recieve $13.99 per person per day.

What should I wear for?

Most of the time casual wear is fine. Elegant wear will be required for some events on the ship.
