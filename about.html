Bio

My name is pronounced in · nay · ka. It's Nigerian in origin and means of a famous or prominent mother. I was born and raised in Arkansas. I love it here. I can't really imagine living anywhere else especially since most of my family is here. I would like to go on a cruise someday to Alaska. Outside the USA, I would like to visit places such as South Korea, Japan, and Australia.

I graduated from UALR in December 2021 with my bachelor's in Web Design and Development. I decided on my degree in web design with the help of my advisor. Since I have a fondness for drawing and computers, she felt that it suited me. I've been on this path ever since.
